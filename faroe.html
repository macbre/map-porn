Cattle grids

Owcze bramki

Cattle grids (or rulluportur in Faroese) are quite common on Faroe Islands. With some 70 thousand sheep grazing in the Islands some means to control their journeys are necessary.

Most of the gates are maintained by Landsverk - the administrator of the Faroese transportation network.
